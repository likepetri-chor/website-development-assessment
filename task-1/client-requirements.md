As stated in [Task 1(1)](assessment-outline.md#Task%201) a list of the client's requirements need to be established; while a general list was provided in the brief, it's quite vague in some areas which isn't very helpful when designing a website in a professional environment. However, using additional information provided we can infer and piece together what they're looking for.

---

- Targeted towards 13-19 year olds.
- Website must be responsive to mobile devices.
- Consist of three web pages.
- Information about the organisation itself.
- Information about an upcoming event.
- A member's personal story.
- An e-mail link to contact said organisation.
- A form to join the organisation.
- Interactive image slider with captions.
- Resource section with filter functionality.
- Positive representations of the organisation and its members.
- Website must include accessibility features.
